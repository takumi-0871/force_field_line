Detailed Description

Evaluate poynting flux in physical space
$omp parallel is required to use these routines. 

      subroutine cal_electric_field_smp                               &
     &         (nnod, coef_d, current, uxb, e_field)
      subroutine cal_poynting_flux_smp                                &
     &         (nnod, coef_d, current, uxb, b_field, poynting)
      subroutine cal_rtp_magnetic_streach                             &
     &         (nnod, nri, nth, a_r_1d_rtp_r, cot_theta_1d_rtp,       &
     &          b_field, u_field, grad_ux, grad_uy, grad_uz,          &
     &          magne_streach)
      subroutine cal_xyz_magnetic_streach(nnod, b_field,              &
     &          grad_ux, grad_uy, grad_uz, magne_streach)

      subroutine cal_rtp_magnetic_streach_tmp                         &
     &         (nnod, nri, nth, a_r_1d_rtp_r, cot_theta_1d_rtp,       &
     &          b_field, u_field, grad_ux, grad_uy, grad_uz,          &
     &          magne_streach)


Parameters
np_smpNumber of SMP processes
nnodNumber of data points
inod_smp_stack(0:np_smp)End address of each SMP process
coef_dCoefficient for magnetic diffusion


Parameters
b_field(nnod,3)Magnetic field \( B_{i} \)
current(nnod,3)Current density \(e_{ijk) \partial_{j} B_{k})\)
uxb(nnod,3)Induction \((e_{ijk) u{j} B_{k})\)
e_field(nnod,3)Electric field \( E_{i} = \sigma^{-1} J_{i} - (e_{ijk) u{j} B_{k})\)
poynting(nnod,3)Poynting flux \((e_{ijk) E{j} B_{k})\)
