module cvt_xyz_vector_2_cyl_smp

Author
H. Matsui
Date
Programmed in June, 2005

Convert symmetric tensor from Cartesian coordinate to cylindrical coordinate

***********************************************************************
*
*   convert vector from certecian coordinate to cylindrical coordinate
*      vs =  vx*cos(phi) + vy*sin(phi)
*      vp = -vx*sin(phi) + vy*cos(phi)
*
***********************************************************************

      subroutine cvt_vector_2_cyl_smp(np_smp, numnod,                 &
     &          inod_smp_stack, vect, v_cyl, xx, yy, rs, a_s)

      subroutine overwrite_vector_2_cyl_smp(np_smp, numnod,           &
     &          inod_smp_stack, vect, xx, yy, rs, a_s)

      subroutine cal_cylinder_r_comp_smp(np_smp, numnod,              &
     &          inod_smp_stack, vect, v_s, xx, yy, rs, a_s)

***********************************************************************


Parameters
np_smpNumber of SMP processes
numnodNumber of data points
inod_smp_stack(0:np_smp)End address of each SMP process
xx(numnod)position in Cartesian coordinate
yy(numnod)position in Cartesian coordinate
zz(numnod)position in Cartesian coordinate
s(numnod)cylindrical radius
a_s(numnod)1 / s


Parameters
vect(numnod,3)vector in Cartesian coordinate


Parameters
v_cyl(numnod,3)vector in cylindrical coordinate
v_s(numnod)s component of vector in Cartesian coordinate
