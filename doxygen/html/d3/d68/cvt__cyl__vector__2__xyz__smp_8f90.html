module cvt_cyl_vector_2_xyz_smp

Author
H. Matsui
Date
Programmed in Oct., 2007

Convert symmetric tensor from cylindrical coordinate to Cartesian coordinate

*********************************************************************
*
*   convert vector from spherical coordinate to certecian coordinate
*      vx =  vs*cos(phi) - vp*sin(phi)
*      vy =  vs*sin(phi) + vp*cos(phi)
*      vz =  vz
*
*********************************************************************

      subroutine cvt_cyl_vect_2_xyz_smp(np_smp, numnod,               &
     &          inod_smp_stack, vect, v_cyl, phi)

      subroutine overwrite_cyl_vect_2_xyz_smp(np_smp, numnod,         &
     &          inod_smp_stack, vect, phi)

      subroutine cvt_cyl_vect_2_x_comp_smp(np_smp, numnod,            &
     &          inod_smp_stack, v_x, v_cyl, phi)
      subroutine cvt_cyl_vect_2_y_comp_smp(np_smp, numnod,            &
     &          inod_smp_stack, v_y, v_cyl, phi)

*********************************************************************


Parameters
np_smpNumber of SMP processes
numnodNumber of data points
inod_smp_stack(0:np_smp)End address of each SMP process
phi(numnod)longitude


Parameters
v_cyl(numnod,3)vector in cylindrical coordinate


Parameters
vect(numnod,3)vector in Cartesian coordinate
v_x(numnod)x component of vector in Cartesian coordinate
v_y(numnod)y component of vector in Cartesian coordinate
v_z(numnod)z component of vector in Cartesian coordinate
