Detailed Description

module cvt_sph_vector_2_xyz_smp

Author
H. Matsui
Date
Programmed in June, 2005

Convert vector from spherical coordinate to Cartesian coordinate

*********************************************************************
*
*   convert vector from spherical coordinate to certecian coordinate
*      vx =  vr*sin(th)*cos(phi) + vt*cos(th)*cos(phi) - vp*sin(phi)
*      vy =  vr*sin(th)*sin(phi) + vt*cos(th)*sin(phi) + vp*cos(phi)
*      vz =  vr*cos(th) - vt*sin(th)
*
*********************************************************************

      subroutine cvt_sph_vect_2_xyz_smp(np_smp, numnod,               &
     &          inod_smp_stack, vect, v_sph, theta, phi)

      subroutine overwrite_sph_vect_2_xyz_smp(np_smp, numnod,         &
     &          inod_smp_stack, vect, theta, phi)

      subroutine cal_sph_2_x_comp_smp(np_smp, numnod, inod_smp_stack, &
     &          v_x, v_sph, theta, phi)
      subroutine cal_sph_2_y_comp_smp(np_smp, numnod, inod_smp_stack, &
     &          v_y, v_sph, theta, phi)
      subroutine cal_sph_2_z_comp_smp(np_smp, numnod, inod_smp_stack, &
     &          v_z, v_sph, theta)

*********************************************************************


Parameters
np_smpNumber of SMP processes
numnodNumber of data points
inod_smp_stack(0:np_smp)End address of each SMP process
theta(numnod)colatitude
phi(numnod)longitude


Parameters
v_sph(numnod,3)vector in spherical coordinate


Parameters
vect(numnod,3)vector in Cartesian coordinate
v_x(numnod)x component of vector in Cartesian coordinate
v_y(numnod)y component of vector in Cartesian coordinate
v_z(numnod)z component of vector in Cartesian coordinate
